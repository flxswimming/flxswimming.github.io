About Me

Hey there! I’m Coach Xavier, Founder and Head Instructor of FLX Swimming. I’m passionate about making a Difference For All Swimmers!

My journey started as a lifeguard at one of Dubai’s most iconic waterparks, where I quickly rose through the ranks to lifeguard supervisor.

But before diving headfirst into teaching swimming, I also took to the skies! For nearly a decade, I worked in aviation, overseeing top-notch service delivery and ensuring everything ran smoothly. This experience sharpened my skills in precision, patience, and understanding people’s unique needs—qualities that now fuel my swimming lessons.

From toddlers testing the waters to adults diving in for the first time, I’ve got a knack for turning fear into fun. My teaching philosophy is simple: swimming should be enjoyable, safe, and tailored to each individual’s pace. That’s why I Bring personalized lessons to the comfort of your doorstep! no stress, no crowds, just you, the water and a splash of laughter.

Mission: To empower All individuals of All Ages to feel Confident, Safe, and joyful in the water through personalized tailored swimming lessons that transform fears into strengths. We believe that swimming is not just a skill. It's a lifelong journey of growth, fitness and fun, taught in a relaxed and supportive environment.

Vision: To become the leading provider of personalized swimming instruction in Dubai, inspiring a community where everyone—from beginners to seasoned swimmers—feels at home in the water. We strive to make swimming accessible, enjoyable, and enriching, one splash at a time for all our swimmers.

So, whether you're looking to perfect your strokes or conquer your fear of deep waters, I’m here to guide you every step of the way. Let’s dive in together and discover the swimmer in you!
